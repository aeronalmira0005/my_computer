Computer parts

different parts of Computer

computer case

The computer case serves critical functions much as skin does for the human body.

Motherboard

called mainboard, main circuit board, or mobo) is the main printed circuit board (PCB)

cpu

central processing unit (CPU), principal part of any digital computer system

RAM

Computer random access memory (RAM) is one of the most fundamental elements of computing.

Computer-Graphics-Card

graphics card is an expansion card for your PC that is responsible for rendering images to the display.

Sound-Card

(as from a microphone or audio tape) and convert it to digital data

HARD - DRIVE

(HDD) is a traditional storage device that uses mechanical platters and a moving read/write

SSD-Drives

A solid-state drive (SSD) is a new generation of storage device used in computers

power Supply

A power supply is an electronic circuit that converts the voltage of an alternating current (AC)

Monitor

A computer monitor is an output device that displays information in pictorial or text form

keybord

A keyboard is for putting information including letters, words and numbers into your computer

MOuse

The mouse is a small, movable device that lets you control a range of things on a computer

print

In computing, a printer is a peripheral machine which makes a persistent representation of graphics or text, usually on paper

scanner

called optical scanner, computer input device that uses a light beam to scan codes, text, or graphic images directly into a computer or computer system.

Speaker

The speaker converts the electric signal of the microphone into the corresponding sound wave
